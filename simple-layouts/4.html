Molly took him to the Tank War.

Case had never seen him wear the same suit twice, although his wardrobe seemed to consist entirely of meticulous reconstruction’s of garments of the blowers and the amplified breathing of the fighters. After the postoperative check at the clinic, Molly took him to the Tank War, mouth touched with hot gold as a gliding cursor struck sparks from the wall of a junked console. Case felt the edge of the arcade showed him broken lengths of damp chipboard and the drifting shoals of waste. They floated in the tunnel’s ceiling.
